Noteworthy
Noteworthy is a stationery brand I came up with to design a visual identity for. The brand was made with a purpose of raising awareness of recycling. Because of this I designed the brand’s identity around the idea of recycling and saving the environment, and also had the idea of making all the bags, business cards etc made fully out of recycled materials.
